About Me

I am currently pursuing a master's degree in Software Engineering. My passions are painting, rockclimbing and cooking. I started rockclimbing and painting about 3 years ago, and they are great outlets that allow my creativity to flow.


My bachelor's degree is in Mechanical engineering but it does not seem to be the right fit for me. While I enjoy some aspects of mechanical engineering like CAD, overall it does not feel like it is working out. This is why I am starting a new journey into Software engineering.


I have been trying out different languages and learning everything I can while still making my studies a top priority. I have tried out C++, Java, Python, Javascript, HTML and CSS. I am still learning what I like most but I am really enjoying front end work because it allows me to utilize my creativity. I am learning it all on my free time and will continue to grow.
